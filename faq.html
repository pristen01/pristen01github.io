Frequently asked Questions How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?
At http://print.hz.nl can you log in with your HZ account and upload a file that you want to print
using your HZ pas by any HZ printers you will be able print them.		 At a HZ printer you can scan your pas and choose scan and the files will be send to your mail box.

How can I buy something on the HZ web shop? Where can I find the latest instructions on what to do when you are sick
